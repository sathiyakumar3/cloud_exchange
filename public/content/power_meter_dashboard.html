Problem Identification

Power Monitoring is one of the primary problems that we tried to solve. In many buildings the power meters data are being collected through a software or even manually and we have reasons to believe the data is not being utilized fully. We tried to solve this problem by using a low cost IoT device -ESP32 to be connected to existing power meters through the meters’ HLI.   We tried to obtain the most relevant data from the meter as much as possible. After serval months of fine tuning, we have developed a powerful dashboard that give you a complete picture of power consumption. We bring all the required points to your fingertips.
